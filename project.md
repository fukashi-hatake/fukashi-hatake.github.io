The final project for the Artificial Intelligence course will be conducted as a group-based project. Students are expected to develop an AI-related project on a topic of their choice and present their work during the final week of the course.

The topic is open, allowing students to explore any area related to artificial intelligence, such as machine learning, deep learning, computer vision, natural language processing, recommendation systems, data analysis, or other AI applications. The main purpose of the final project is to help students reflect on and apply the knowledge, concepts, and skills they have learned throughout the course.

Each group should clearly define a problem, propose an AI-based solution, implement or demonstrate the proposed approach, and explain the results. The final presentation should include the project motivation, methodology, implementation details, results, challenges, and key lessons learned.

This project is an important part of the course evaluation and is designed to encourage creativity, teamwork, problem-solving, and practical understanding of artificial intelligence.
